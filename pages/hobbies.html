Anime

Anime is word for Japanese animation/cartoons, one the reason of calling it "Anime" is because the word cartoons, is usually for children, which is "子供向" in Japanese. Anime could include all type of topic like drama and movie, but in a more exaggerated expression, which is a better way for creator to emphasize things. Gundam would be great example for this. It has different series and most of the series are my favourite.
My most favourite "Gundam 00", it is an anime showed sorrow or tragedy made by war, the cruelness of war and humanity, how people fight to reach their justice and dream, and the good vision of mankind can understand each other and united together.

Video game

Nowadays, most of the people should have the experience of playing mobile games, or even have handheld console like the Nintendo Switch. However, those who truly understand the charm of video game is not much. A movies can let you have a 2 hours vision and audio experience, but video games allow you to "live" in the world for a couple hundred hours. A well made 3A video game is definitely cannot compare to mobile games, the expressiveness is not in the same dimension. And for movie, movie might have better expression, but Immersive experience is something that only video games can do.
One of my favourite video game would be "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt", I would say it's a legend in video games, and not outdated even compare to the 3A games today. The game is based on the Polish writer, Andrzej Sapkowski's masterpiece "The Witcher". The game is set in the Continent, a fantasy world surrounded by parallel dimensions and extra-dimensional worlds and it's talk about how Geralt found his adopted daughter Ciri and how he help her to face her fate of being the chosen one.
